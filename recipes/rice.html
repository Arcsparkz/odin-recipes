Odin Rice!

delicious rice flavoured with garlic

This rice is steamed with salt and infused with garlic and chives to give a delicate but balanced taste

Ingredients

  • 1 cup - basmati rice
  • 2 cups - water
  • 3 cloves - garlic
  • 2 teaspoons - chives

Steps

  1. place rice into rice cooker
  2. add 2 cups water to rice cooker
  3. crush garlic and add to rice cooker
  4. add the chives let Cook
